# Managing Email Campaigns

View and manage all your email campaigns from the organization dashboard.

## Viewing Campaigns

Your organization dashboard shows all campaigns:

**Draft Campaigns:**
- Not yet sent
- Can be edited
- Can be deleted

**Sent Campaigns:**
- Already sent to contacts
- Shows send date
- Displays statistics
- Cannot be edited

**Scheduled Campaigns:**
- Waiting to send
- Shows scheduled date/time
- Can be unscheduled (cancelled)
- Can be edited before send time

## Editing Campaigns

### Editing Drafts

1. Click on a draft campaign
2. Make changes in the editor
3. Changes auto-save
4. Click Send when ready

### Editing Scheduled Campaigns

1. Find the scheduled campaign
2. Click to open editor
3. Make changes
4. Changes auto-save
5. Campaign sends at scheduled time

### Sent Campaigns

Sent campaigns cannot be edited. Create a new campaign if you need to send updated content.

## Deleting Campaigns

1. Click on the campaign
2. Click Delete
3. Confirm deletion

> Cannot delete scheduled campaigns. Unschedule first, then delete.

## Organizing Campaigns

Use clear campaign names to stay organized:

- Include date or purpose
- Use consistent naming conventions
- Add descriptions for complex campaigns

**Examples:**
- "Newsletter - December 2024"
- "Event Reminder - Town Hall"
- "Welcome Series - Email 1"

## Campaign Statistics

View performance metrics for sent campaigns:

**Delivery:**
- Total sent
- Delivered
- Bounced

**Engagement:**
- Opens
- Unique opens
- Clicks
- Unique clicks

**Unsubscribes:**
- Number of unsubscribes
- Unsubscribe rate

Click on a sent campaign to see detailed statistics.
