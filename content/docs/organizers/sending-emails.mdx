# Sending Email Campaigns

Send your email campaigns to all contacts or specific segments.

## Before Sending

Make sure you have:

- Completed email design
- Set subject line and preview text
- Configured from name
- Added required content blocks

## Sending Options

### Send Now

Emails are sent immediately to all recipients.

### Schedule for Later

Choose a specific date and time to send emails automatically.

## Choosing Recipients

### All Contacts

Send to everyone in your Master List (excluding unsubscribed contacts).

### Specific Segment

Select a saved segment to target specific contacts.

> Unsubscribed contacts are automatically excluded from all campaigns.

## Send Dialog

When you click Send Email:

### 1. Select Recipients

Choose:
- All contacts (default)
- Specific segment from dropdown

Contact count shows how many people will receive the email.

### 2. Choose Timing

**Send Now:**
- Emails send immediately
- Cannot be cancelled once sent

**Schedule:**
- Select date and time
- Can be cancelled before scheduled time
- Appears in scheduled emails list

### 3. Test Email (Optional)

Send a test email to yourself before sending to contacts:

1. Enter your email address
2. Click Send Test
3. Check your inbox
4. Verify content, links, and formatting

### 4. Send

Click Send Now or Schedule to confirm.

## After Sending

### View Sent Emails

Go to your organization dashboard to see:
- All sent campaigns
- Send date and time
- Recipient count
- Email statistics (opens, clicks)

### Scheduled Emails

Scheduled campaigns appear in your email list with:
- Scheduled date and time
- Option to unschedule (cancel)

To cancel a scheduled email:
1. Find the email in your list
2. Click Unschedule
3. Confirm cancellation

## Email Statistics

After sending, track:
- Open rate
- Click rate
- Bounce rate
- Unsubscribe rate

View detailed stats on the email details page.

## Best Practices

- Always send a test email first
- Review preview before sending
- Double-check recipient selection (all vs segment)
- Verify all links work
- Check subject line and preview text
- Schedule during optimal send times

## Troubleshooting

### Send Button Disabled

Check:
- Subject line is filled in
- Email has content
- From name is set
- Valid recipients selected

### No Recipients Found

- Ensure your Master List has contacts
- Check segment has contacts if using segment filter
- Verify contacts are not all unsubscribed

### Test Email Not Received

- Check spam folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Wait a few minutes (may be delayed)
