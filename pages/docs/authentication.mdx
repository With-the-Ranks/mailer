# Authentication

Learn how to manage your Mailer account, from signing up to securing it with two-factor authentication.

## Sign Up

Creating a new Mailer account is quick and easy.

<Steps>

### Navigate to the Sign Up page

Go to [https://app.withtheranks.coop/register](https://app.withtheranks.coop/register) or click "Sign Up" from the login page.

### Enter your email address

Provide a valid email address that you have access to. This will be used for account verification and important notifications.

### Create a strong password

Your password should be:

- At least 8 characters long
- Include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Not be easily guessable

### Submit the form

Click "Register" to create your account.

### Check your email

You'll receive a verification email within a few minutes.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  **Important:** You must verify your email address before you can log in to
  your account.
</Callout>

## Email Verification

After signing up, you need to verify your email address to activate your account.

<Steps>

### Check your inbox

Look for an email from Mailer with the subject "Verify your email address."

<Callout type="warning">
  Didn't receive the email? Check your spam folder or request a new verification
  email from the login page.
</Callout>

### Click the verification link

The email contains a unique verification link. Click it to verify your account.

### Confirmation

You'll be redirected to a confirmation page, and your account will be activated.

</Steps>

### Resending Verification Email

If you didn't receive the verification email:

1. Go to the login page
2. Try to log in with your credentials
3. You'll see a message prompting you to verify your email
4. Click "Resend" to get a new verification email

## Login

Once your email is verified, you can log in to your account.

<Steps>

### Go to the login page

Navigate to [https://app.withtheranks.coop/login](https://app.withtheranks.coop/login)

### Enter your credentials

- **Email:** The email address you registered with
- **Password:** Your account password

### Click "Login"

You'll be redirected to your dashboard.

</Steps>

<Callout type="error">
  **Login Failed?** Make sure your email is verified and your credentials are
  correct. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option.
</Callout>

## Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password, you can easily reset it.

<Steps>

### Click "Forgot Password"

On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link.

### Enter your email

Provide the email address associated with your account.

### Check your email

You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes.

### Click the reset link

The email contains a unique link to reset your password. Click it.

### Create a new password

Enter and confirm your new password.

### Log in

Use your new password to log in to your account.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Password reset links expire after 24 hours for security reasons.
</Callout>
